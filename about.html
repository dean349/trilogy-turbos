The Man Behind The Machine

Decades of Local Expertise

Heron didn't fall into this trade — he was raised in it. What began as a teenager tearing down engines in a South Florida driveway grew into more than two decades of hands-on mastery across every corner of the automotive world. From dealership service bays to independent performance shops, Heron built a reputation as the mechanic other mechanics call when a job gets complicated.

His obsession is precision. Whether he's dialing in the air-fuel curve on a vintage Holley carburetor or reading live boost-pressure data off a European twin-scroll turbocharger, Heron treats every vehicle as a system to be understood — not a ticket to be closed. That philosophy is exactly why classic collectors, daily commuters, and import enthusiasts alike trust him with keys they'd never hand to anyone else.
